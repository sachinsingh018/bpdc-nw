Networkqy ($NQY) Whitepaper Version 1.0

4 August 2025

Networkqy is an AI-powered professional networking platform where individuals can build trusted connections, showcase their skills, and grow their careers in a gamified, transparent way. To enable this, we introduce the $NQY token, a utility token designed to reward meaningful engagement, power trust mechanisms, and unlock premium features for individuals within the Networkqy ecosystem.

The Problem

No Rewards for Engagement

Current platforms profit from user activity without giving value back to contributors.

Static Networking

Existing systems are resume-based and lack real-time, AI-driven personalization.

Fake & Low-Trust Profiles

Inflated credentials and bots reduce the quality of connections.

Lack of Motivation

Professionals and students don't have an incentive to actively engage, mentor, or share knowledge.

The Solution: Networkqy + $NQY

Networkqy redefines professional networking with AI + Tokenized Incentives:

  • Earn for Engagement
  • Trust Through AuraQY
  • AI-Powered Matching
  • Gamified Experience

Earn for Engagement

Users are rewarded in $NQY for verified actions such as posting, mentoring, event participation, referrals, and knowledge sharing.

Trust Through AuraQY

An on-chain trust score enhanced by staking $NQY, ensuring profiles are credible and spam is minimized.

AI-Powered Matching

Smart recommendations for people, opportunities, and communities.

Gamified Experience

Badges, streaks, and achievements powered by $NQY incentives.

$NQY Token Utility

$NQY is the digital fuel of the Networkqy B2C ecosystem.
